1v1.lol: The Best Online Battle & Building Game

1v1.lol is a fast-paced multiplayer shooter that combines battle royale action with strategic building mechanics. Players compete in intense 1v1 duels or large-scale battles while constructing defenses to outsmart opponents.

How to Play 1v1.lol

To play 1v1.lol, choose a game mode (1v1, 2v2, or Battle Royale), then use WASD to move and your mouse to aim/shoot. Master building by pressing keys (Q, E, R, F) to place walls, ramps, and floors for cover. Collect better weapons and eliminate opponents to win!
